Full reference

🏀 Pool Structure

  • Each of the 16 players is assigned 2 teams from the Round of 32 field.
  • Left half = South/East regions. Right half = West/Midwest regions.
  • Each player owns one team from each side of the bracket — they can't face each other until the Final Four.
  • Bracket follows real NCAA tournament seedings and matchups.

🎯 Spread Mechanic

  • Winning team covers the spread → owner retains the team.
  • Winning team does NOT cover → losing team's owner takes over the winner next round.
  • Push (tie vs spread) → no takeover, original owner retains.
